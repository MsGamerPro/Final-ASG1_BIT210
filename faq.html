Frequently Asked Questions


Why can't I log in to my Account? A pop-up message tells me the phone number has been registered under an existing account?


This occurs when user created two or more accounts with us. To resolve this issue, simply disable one of the accounts by providing us the Name, Email and Contact Number registered to the selected account for BookDoc to handle the issue.

How do I view my daily steps record/history?


Go to our Activ page on the app to view current Average Daily Steps for the particular month.

If you wish to view your steps history, you will need to open the original source app e.g. Samsung Health, Google Fit or Apple Health.

I cannot enter the store ID to redeem my e-voucher. Why?


Store ID(s) are to be entered by the Reward Partner's Staff to invalidate the e-voucher.

In order to redeem an e-voucher, you are required to visit the Reward Partner outlet (refer to T&C for the list of participating outlets).

Open the app and show the e-voucher to the staff in order to get the discount which will then dissapear from your e-wallet when succesfully redeemed

I am interested in the Premium Programme. How do I join?


Please share with us these particular details to further assist you on your query:

  • - Company's name and Company's address
  • - Person in charge for us to contact (HR)
  • - Company's contact number

Which wearable device can sync with BookDoc?


  • - Fitbit
  • - Garmin
  • - Jawbone
  • - Mi Band/Mi Band 2 (via Google Fit and data have to be updated on a daily basis)

I am not receiving any Pin Code through SMS to verify my phone number. Why?


BookDoc relies on a third-party service provider which some disruptions could sometimes happen. However, we hope to get this issue sorted out soon.

As for now, please restart your phone and try to generate the pin code again and ensure your connection is decent enough during the process
